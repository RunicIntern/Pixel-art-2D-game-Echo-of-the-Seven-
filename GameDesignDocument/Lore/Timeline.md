# Timeline of Aurion – Complete Historical Overview

This document presents the chronological history of Aurion, from the formation of the Seven Domains to the present age in which the events of *Echo of the Seven* take place.

The timeline is divided into four major eras:

1. Pre-Runic Era (Era of the Ancient Kings)  
2. The Runic Cataclysm  
3. The Shadow Era  
4. Era of Echo (current age)

---

# 0. Before the Beginning – The Unspoken Time
**Timeframe: unknown**

- The Seven Domains emerge as cosmic forces without form.  
- Their interactions create the foundations of future reality.  
- No matter, no time, no consciousness exists yet.

This age is known only through fragmented visions of the Guardians.

---

# 1. Pre-Runic Era – Age of the Ancient Kings  
**Timeframe: millions of years**

## 1.1 Birth of the Ancient Kings
- The Seven Domains gain self-awareness.  
- Each Domain takes physical form, becoming one of the Ancient Kings.  
- The Pantheon is established.

## 1.2 Shaping of Aurion
- **Igralon (Creation)** forms mountains, oceans and continents.  
- **Elyndor (Space)** establishes borders, paths and spatial laws.  
- **Vaeloria (Life)** creates the first flora and fauna.  
- **Aren-Thul (Time)** introduces cycles and the flow of ages.  
- **Seryth (Change)** triggers evolution and adaptation.  
- **Korveth (Strength)** stabilises natural forces.  
- **Melyon (Knowledge)** encodes the world through runic laws.

The world flourishes under their combined will.

## 1.3 Creation of the Guardians
- The Kings craft seven Guardians to maintain balance between Domains.  
- Guardians serve as interpreters and mediators of the cosmic laws.

## 1.4 First Civilisations
- Early pre-cataclysmic societies emerge.  
- Primitive runic magic is discovered.  
- The world enters an age of order and harmony.

---

# 2. Rise of Conflict  
**Timeframe: tens of thousands of years**

## 2.1 Domains Begin to Clash
- Philosophies diverge:
  - Life clashes with Change.  
  - Strength opposes Knowledge.  
  - Time conflicts with Creation.  
- Space attempts to mediate.

## 2.2 Worldwide Instability
- Mutations spread across flora and fauna.  
- Time fractures appear.  
- Spatial distortions form.  
- Natural laws begin to unravel.

## 2.3 Collapse of the Council of Kings
- Disagreements escalate.  
- Each King follows their own doctrine.  
- Guardians are dismissed from decision-making.

The world enters an irreversible spiral.

---

# 3. The Runic Cataclysm  
**Timeframe: the event itself lasts one day; consequences span a millennium**

## 3.1 Melyon’s Revelation
- Melyon (Knowledge) concludes that the conflict between Domains cannot be resolved.  
- He foresees the collapse of the world if the system is not rebuilt.  
- Only Elyndor (Space) understands the severity of the threat.

## 3.2 Alliance of Two
- Melyon and Elyndor develop a drastic solution:  
  **the Runic Crystal**, capable of absorbing all Domains.  
- Their cooperation is born not from agreement, but necessity.

## 3.3 Collapse of the Pantheon
- The Crystal activates.  
- The Domains fight within it.  
- Kings are consumed:
  - Aren-Thul attempts to freeze time → absorbed.  
  - Vaeloria shields others → absorbed.  
  - Igralon attempts counter-creation → fails.  
  - Korveth attacks the Crystal → destroyed.  
  - Seryth changes form endlessly → overwhelmed.  
  - Elyndor tries to teleport the Crystal beyond Aurion → space collapses.  
  - **Melyon enters voluntarily**, accepting the consequences.

## 3.4 The Detonation
- The Runic Crystal explodes.  
- Reality fractures.  
- The Ancient Kings perish.

## 3.5 Aftermath
- Millions of runes scatter across the world.  
- Echo Runes are formed from the largest fragments of divine essence.  
- Landscapes are reshaped.  
- Early civilisations collapse.  
- Magic becomes unstable and widespread.  
- Anomalies appear in every region.

The world enters the Age of Echoes.

---

# 4. The Shadow Era  
**Timeframe: 0–1500 years after the Cataclysm**

## 4.1 Loss of Ancient Knowledge
- Survivors fail to understand what occurred.  
- Runic artefacts are feared.  
- Lore of the Ancient Kings is twisted into myth.

## 4.2 Guardians in Exile
- Severed from their Domains, Guardians lose much of their power.  
- Their memories become fragmented.  
- They hide among mortals in humanoid forms.

## 4.3 Birth of New Races
Through mutation, magic and the remnants of pre-cataclysmic species:

- humans,  
- elves,  
- dwarves,  
- orcs,  
- hybrids,  
- and countless monsters emerge.

## 4.4 Rise of Early Religions
- Mortals misinterpret Guardian encounters as divine revelations.  
- Primitive cults and early pantheons form around these misunderstandings.

---

# 5. Era of Echo – The Present Age  
**Timeframe: current era in the game**

## 5.1 Awakening of Echo Runes
- Echo Runes begin to pulse with renewed energy.  
- Anomalies intensify.  
- Magic grows unstable.

## 5.2 Aurion Reborn
- Cities rise in regions shaped by runic scars.  
- Trade, conflict and exploration flourish.  
- Monsters grow stronger.

## 5.3 Guardians Reemerge
- They watch mortals quietly from the shadows.  
- Some appear in visions or dreams.  
- They search for individuals capable of preventing a second Cataclysm.

## 5.4 A New Threat Rises
According to the Guardians:

> **The Runic Cataclysm was only phase one.  
Something that survived the merging of the Domains is beginning to awaken.**

The Age of Echoes begins.
